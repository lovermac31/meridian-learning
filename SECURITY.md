# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in Jurassic English
(jurassicenglish.com) or related World Wise Learning systems, please report it
**privately** — do not open a public GitHub issue.

- **Email:** legal@worldwiselearning.com (primary) or info@jurassicenglish.com
- Please include steps to reproduce, affected URL(s), and a short impact description.
- We aim to acknowledge reports within **2 business days**.

We support responsible disclosure and will not pursue action against researchers
who act in good faith, avoid privacy violations and service disruption, and give
us reasonable time to remediate before any public discussion.

## Scope

- **In scope:** `jurassicenglish.com`, its subdomains, and the serverless API.
- **Out of scope:** third-party services we integrate with (please report those
  to the respective vendor).

See also [`/.well-known/security.txt`](public/.well-known/security.txt).
