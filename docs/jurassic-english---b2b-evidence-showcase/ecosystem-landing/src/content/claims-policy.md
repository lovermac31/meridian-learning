# Jurassic English™ Claims Policy

## Rule 1
Do not claim guaranteed exam results unless supported by verified pilot data.

## Rule 2
Use “designed to support,” “structured to help,” and “tracks evidence of” language.

## Rule 3
Evidence must come from student artifacts, diagnostic samples, portfolio records, teacher rubrics, parent feedback, or anonymized cohort results.

## Rule 4
Do not publish student data without permission.

## Rule 5
Do not compare Jurassic English™ to named competitors without verified evidence.

## Rule 6
Keep school-facing and student-facing CTAs separate.
